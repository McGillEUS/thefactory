Exporting your PCB

This section explains how to export and send your PCB so it can be milled at the lab. The Factory offers custom PCB milling with a maximum of 2 layers.
The accepted file types are:

  • Eagle BRD (.brd)
  • Gerber RS-274X (.gtl and .grb)
  • G-Code (.nc and .tap)
  • SVG (.svg)
